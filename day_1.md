## What is Data Science?  
- Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data.
- It combines aspects of statistics, computer science, and domain expertise to analyze and interpret data.
- The goal is to make informed decisions or predictions based on data.

---

## Why is Data Science important?  
- Helps businesses make data-driven decisions.
- Enables prediction of future trends through machine learning.
- Automates and improves processes by analyzing large datasets.
- Supports innovation in various sectors like healthcare, finance, marketing, and more.

---

## Common Steps in a Data Science Project  
- Data Collection: Gathering raw data from various sources.
- Data Cleaning: Handling missing data, removing duplicates, correcting errors.
- Data Exploration: Understanding data through statistics and visualization.
- Modeling: Applying machine learning algorithms to find patterns.
- Evaluation: Testing model accuracy and performance.
- Deployment: Putting models into production for real-world use.
